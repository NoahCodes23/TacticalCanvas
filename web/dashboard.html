How to drive it

Hand: pinch thumb and index finger together to grab the nearest player. Move while pinched, then open the pinch to drop. The cursor sits between both fingertips at all times. Brief tracking gaps are tolerated, and a nearby piece snaps to the pinch when the cursor is about one piece-width away. Grabbing auto-pauses.

Draw: while paused, hold your extended index and middle fingertips together, with either side of the hand facing the camera. Move the red pen cursor to paint freehand lines, circles, or shapes. Separate or lower the fingers to end one stroke. Hold index, middle, and ring together to erase wherever the white eraser passes. Hold all five fingers open briefly to clear every drawing. Drawings also clear when playback resumes.

Mouse: drag players on this pitch — always works, camera or not.

Calibrate: open the projector on the field, then press Calibrate field. Keep the camera and projector still while all six field markers are collected.
