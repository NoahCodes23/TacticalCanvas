How to drive it

Hand: pinch thumb and index finger together to grab the nearest player. Move while pinched, then open the pinch to drop. The cursor sits between both fingertips and grabbing auto-pauses.

Mouse: drag players on this pitch — always works, camera or not.

Calibrate: open the projector on the field, then press Calibrate field. Keep the camera and projector still while all four corner markers are collected.
