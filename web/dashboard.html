How to drive it

Hand: one finger out = grab the nearest player and drag. Two fingers (or an open hand) = drop. Grabbing auto-pauses.

Mouse: drag players on this pitch — always works, camera or not.

Calibrate: show markers → click corners 1,2,3,4 in the camera window → press s.
